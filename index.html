Summer Workshop on Statistics and Data Analytics (SDA)

This workshop is organized by the HKU Business School. We aim to bring together a few leading researchers in statistics and data science to discuss interesting researches and improve in-person interactions of the data science community.

Time: June 3, 2024.

Location: Business School, The University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong.

Accommodation and Transportation: Detailed information about accommodation, transportation and some useful links can be found here.

Wi-Fi: A visitor Wi-Fi network is available: Wi-Fi.HK via HKU (no authentication required).

Organizers

The SDA Summer Workshop is organized by the Statistics Group within the Innovation and Information Management Management Department of the HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong. For further information, please contact the SDA organizers.

Accessibility

Individuals with disabilities who need accommodations to attend this event should contact the SDA organizers. It is kindly requested that individuals requiring accommodations or auxiliary aidsm such as sign language interpreters and alternative format materials, notify the conference organizers at least 7 days prior to the beginning of the event. Every reasonable effort will be made to provide reasonable accommodations in an effective and timely manner.

Code of Conduct

The SDA Conference is a forum for community-building and scholarly exchange, and we hope to foster a welcoming and positive environment for everyone. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or abuse will not be tolerated. As a general code of conduct, we will adopt the ACM Policy Against Harassment. Since the event will be held on the grounds of The University of Hong Kong (HKU), participants should also be aware of all applicable HKU policies. To report an incident or discuss any concerns, please contact the SDA organizers.
